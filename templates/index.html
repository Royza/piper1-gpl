📊 Open TensorBoard

About TensorBoard

TensorBoard provides visualization tools for machine learning experimentation:

  • Scalars: Track loss, learning rate, and other metrics over time
  • Images: View spectrograms and training visualizations
  • Audio: Listen to generated audio samples during training
  • Histograms: Monitor weight and gradient distributions

Start TensorBoard before beginning training to capture all metrics from the beginning.
